How to Visit

By Car

Our museum is easily accessible by car. Free parking is available in our dedicated parking lot. Follow signs from the main highway to Museum Avenue.

By Public Transportation

Take the Metro line 5 and get off at "Museum Station." The museum is just a 5-minute walk from the station. Bus routes 12, 34, and 56 also stop near our entrance.

By Taxi/Rideshare

Drop off points are available at the main entrance. Uber, Lyft, and local taxi services all service our location regularly.

By Bike

Secure bike racks are available at the entrance. The museum is located on the scenic River Park Trail, perfect for bike commuting.
