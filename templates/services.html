Services Offered

The Museum of History provides a variety of services to ensure a comfortable, inclusive, and enriching visit for all visitors.

☕ Museum Café

Enjoy coffee, beverages, and light snacks in our café, located near the main entrance.

Opening Hours: 09:00 – 18:00

🛍️ Gift Shop

Purchase souvenirs, books, replicas, and educational materials inspired by our exhibitions.

Payment: Cash & Cards accepted

🎧 Guided Tours

Guided tours are available for individuals, schools, and groups, led by professional museum guides.

Languages: Greek & English

♿ Accessibility Services

The museum is fully accessible to visitors with disabilities, including ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms.

Additional: Wheelchairs available on request

🎒 Educational Programs

Special programs and workshops are organized for schools, universities, and educational institutions.

Booking: Advance reservation required

📶 Free Wi-Fi

Free wireless internet access is available throughout the museum premises.
